Is AuraAI against OnlyFans terms of service?

No. AuraAI operates similar to hiring chat assistants, which is a common practice. Our system securely integrates with OnlyFans' official API where available, and uses advanced methods that respect platform policies where API access is limited.

How natural do the AI conversations sound?

Incredibly natural. Our AI is trained on millions of conversations and customized to match your unique communication style. Subscribers consistently report they can't tell the difference between the AI and the creator. The AI learns from your previous messages and content to maintain authentic, on-brand communication.

How much time will I save using AuraAI?

Most creators report saving 20-30 hours per week on subscriber messaging. The exact time savings depend on your subscriber count and engagement rate. Our customers typically see a 90% reduction in time spent on routine conversations while actually increasing revenue.

Is my account information secure?

Absolutely. Security is our top priority. We use military-grade encryption and never store your OnlyFans password. Our integration uses secure authentication methods that comply with the highest security standards. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest, and we are fully GDPR compliant.

Can I still chat personally when I want to?

Yes! You have complete control over when the AI is active. You can set rules for which types of messages the AI should handle and which should be flagged for your personal attention. You can also seamlessly take over any conversation at any time. The system is designed to enhance your presence, not replace it.
